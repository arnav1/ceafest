CEAFest 2015: Workshops

STAAD Workshop

INTRODUCTION

STAAD stands for Structural Analysis And Design.STAAD Pro is a structural analysis and design computer program used for steel, concrete structures. It is used for 3D model generation, analysis and multi-material design. It is one of the software applications created to help structural engineers automate their tasks, to remove the tedious and long procedures of the manual methods.
