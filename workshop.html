STAAD PRO

INTRODUCTION:

STAAD stands for Structural Analysis And Design.STAAD Pro is a structural analysis and design computer program used for steel, concrete structures. It is used for 3D model generation, analysis and multi-material design.
It is one of the software applications created to help structural engineers automate their tasks, to remove the tedious and long procedures of the manual methods.

TEKLA

INTRODUCTION:

Tekla Structures is 3D building information modeling (BIM) software used in the building and construction industries for steel and concrete detailing. It enables us to create and manage 3D structural models in concrete or steel, and guides them through the process from concept to fabrication.
